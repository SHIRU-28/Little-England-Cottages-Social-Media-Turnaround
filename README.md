# Little England Cottages: Social Media Turnaround Case Study

**Role:** Social Media Manager & Content Strategist  
**Duration:** 6 Months (April 2025 – October 2025)  

## Overview
A comprehensive portfolio case study detailing the end-to-end social media turnaround for Little England Cottages, a luxury holiday and residential community in Nuwara Eliya, Sri Lanka. 

## Key Results
- **+628.6%** increase in content output
- **+88.6%** growth in Facebook views
- **+280.6%** increase in content interactions
- Scaled total audience to **68,657+** combined followers
